## Common Use Cases Reported by Customers

### First-Time Parents

First-time parents most frequently cite the emotional milestone aspect: hearing their baby's heartbeat for the first time between OB appointments. Many describe this as one of the most memorable experiences of their pregnancy.

### Parents with Pregnancy Anxiety

Parents experiencing pregnancy anxiety — particularly those with previous losses, high-risk pregnancies, or long IVF journeys — frequently cite the HeartBeats™ doppler as providing meaningful daily reassurance. The ability to quickly confirm the presence of a heartbeat between appointments reduces the anxiety of the inter-appointment gap.

### Partners

Partners frequently receive the HeartBeats™ as a gift and use it as their primary way of connecting with the pregnancy. The audible heartbeat provides a tangible, immediate experience of the developing baby.

### Experienced Parents

Parents on their second or third pregnancy use HeartBeats™ to involve older siblings — letting a toddler or older child listen to "the baby's heartbeat" is a popular way to introduce the sibling relationship.
