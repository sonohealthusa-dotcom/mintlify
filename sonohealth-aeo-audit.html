What's Broken or Missing

1. sonohealth.com has NO llms.txt

Your main commercial site returns a 404 for /llms.txt. Only docs.sonohealth.com has one. Since AI bots crawl your shop domain too, you're missing a chance to feed them structured product info directly.

2. No Product Schema on Product Pages

The Spectrum 5 product page at sonohealth.com/product/magnesium-complex-spectrum-5/ has basic Product schema (name, price, SKU) via Rank Math, but is missing AggregateRating and Review schema. This is the #1 reason Forest Leaf wins AI recommendations — their Shopify/Yotpo integration automatically generates this:

  // ❌ No Review array
  // ❌ No FAQ schema

    "ratingValue": "4.8",
    "reviewCount": "134"

  // ✅ Rich snippets in search results

3. No FAQ Schema Anywhere

Your docs site has extensive FAQ content on multiple pages (/magnesium/faq, /fetal-doppler/faq, /nebulizer/faq) plus dedicated question pages, but none of this is marked up with FAQPage schema. FAQ schema is one of the most powerful signals for AI answer engines.

4. Product Page Returns 404

sonohealth.com/product/spectrum-5-magnesium-complex/ returns 404 — the actual URL is /product/magnesium-complex-spectrum-5/. If any external links or AI training data reference the wrong URL, they hit a dead end.

5. No llms-full.txt

Your docs site serves llms.txt (summary) but not llms-full.txt (complete content). The llms-full.txt specification lets AI models ingest your entire knowledge base in one request without crawling 102 pages individually.

6. Review Volume Gap

Spectrum 5 has 34 reviews (5.0 stars). Forest Leaf has 134 reviews (4.8 stars). AI models weight review volume heavily because it signals market validation. Even with a perfect rating, 34 reviews looks like a newer/smaller product to an AI.

Action Plan (Priority Order)

CRITICAL — Do This Week

1. Add AggregateRating + Review Schema to Product Pages

This is the single highest-impact change. Your WordPress/Rank Math setup generates Product schema but omits ratings. You need to add AggregateRating to every product page.

How: In Rank Math SEO → Schema Templates → Edit your Product schema and add:

"aggregateRating": {
  "@type": "AggregateRating",
  "ratingValue": "5.0",
  "reviewCount": "34",
  "bestRating": "5"
}

Alternatively, install a review plugin (like Yotpo, Judge.me, or WooCommerce Product Reviews Pro) that auto-generates this schema from real reviews. This is how Forest Leaf does it automatically.

CRITICAL — Do This Week

2. Submit Both Domains to Bing Webmaster Tools

ChatGPT Search uses Bing's index exclusively. If your pages aren't in Bing, ChatGPT literally cannot recommend you.

Steps:

  • Go to bing.com/webmasters
  • Add both sonohealth.com and docs.sonohealth.com
  • Submit sitemaps: sonohealth.com/sitemap_index.xml and docs.sonohealth.com/sitemap.xml
  • Use "URL Submission" to manually submit your top 10 product/comparison pages
  • Fix any crawl errors Bing reports (like the duplicate h1 issue you already fixed)
CRITICAL — Do This Week

HIGH — Do Within 2 Weeks

4. Add FAQ Schema to FAQ Pages

Your Mintlify docs have rich FAQ content but no FAQPage schema markup. Mintlify supports custom <head> metadata — add JSON-LD FAQPage schema to each FAQ page. This makes your answers directly extractable by AI engines.

For the three main FAQ pages, add structured FAQ schema. You can also add it to each "Common Questions" page since those are already in Q&A format.

HIGH — Do Within 2 Weeks

5. Generate llms-full.txt for docs.sonohealth.com

Your docs site already has llms.txt (summary). Create llms-full.txt that contains the full text of every page in markdown format. This lets AI models ingest your entire knowledge base in a single request.

Mintlify may auto-generate this — check your response headers. Your current pages already return a Link header with </llms.txt> and </llms-full.txt> references.

HIGH — Do Within 2 Weeks

6. Submit to Google Search Console

Gemini and Google AI Overviews pull from Google's index. Submit both domains and sitemaps to Google Search Console if you haven't already. Request indexing for your most important pages.

MEDIUM — Do Within 1 Month

7. Grow Review Count to 100+

34 reviews vs Forest Leaf's 134 is the biggest non-technical gap. AI models treat review volume as a proxy for product legitimacy.

Tactics: Post-purchase email sequence asking for reviews, Amazon review request through Seller Central, include a review card in product packaging, offer a small incentive (discount on next purchase) for leaving honest reviews.

MEDIUM — Do Within 1 Month

8. Build External Brand Mentions

AI platforms weight external mentions 3.2x more than on-site content. Get SonoHealth mentioned on health blogs, supplement review sites, Reddit threads, and YouTube reviews. Each third-party mention reinforces your brand entity in AI training data.

MEDIUM — Do Within 1 Month

9. Add Product Schema to Docs Site via Metadata

Your docs pages about products don't carry Product schema. Mintlify supports custom metadata in frontmatter. Add structured data to key pages like /magnesium/overview, /magnesium/buy, etc., so AI crawlers associate your documentation with purchasable products.

ONGOING

10. Keep Content Fresh

AI systems have a strong recency bias. Update your most important docs pages every 2-3 months — even minor updates signal freshness. Add new comparison pages, update review counts, refresh clinical data references.
