If you suffer from seasonal allergies, pet dander sensitivity, or dust mite reactions, an air purifier can make a measurable difference -- but only if it filters the right particles at the right efficiency. Here is what to look for and why the SonoHealth AirPro is built for allergy relief.

## Common Airborne Allergens

| Allergen | Particle Size |
|---|---|
| Pollen | 10--100 &micro;m |
| Mold spores | 2--20 &micro;m |
| Dust mite debris | 0.5--50 &micro;m |
| Pet dander | 0.5--10 &micro;m |
| Smoke / fine particulate | 0.1--2.5 &micro;m |
| VOCs (off-gassing, cleaning products) | Gaseous (requires carbon filtration) |

Most allergens are larger than 1 micron, but the smallest fragments of dander, mold, and smoke particles drop well below that threshold. A purifier that only handles large particles leaves the most irritating ones in the air.

## Why HEPA 14 Matters for Allergy Sufferers

Standard "True HEPA" (HEPA 13) filters capture 99.95% of particles at 0.3 microns. HEPA 14 raises that to **99.995%** -- a tenfold reduction in particles that slip through. Over hours of continuous operation, that difference adds up significantly.

For allergy sufferers, HEPA 14 means:

- **Fewer breakthrough particles** reaching your nose, throat, and lungs.
- **Better capture of ultrafine allergen fragments** that trigger immune responses.
- **More effective removal of smoke and wildfire particles** during high-pollen seasons when air quality is already compromised.

## The Role of Activated Carbon

Allergens are not just particles. Volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from cleaning products, new furniture, paints, and adhesives can trigger allergy-like symptoms including headaches, nasal congestion, and eye irritation. HEPA filters do not capture gases -- that requires **activated carbon adsorption**.

The AirPro's third filtration stage uses activated carbon to neutralize VOCs, household odors, and chemical irritants that a HEPA-only purifier would miss entirely.

## UV-C Sterilization and Allergens

Mold spores and bacteria can colonize damp filter media over time. The AirPro's built-in UV-C light sterilizes captured biological contaminants, preventing them from multiplying inside the unit and being released back into your air.
