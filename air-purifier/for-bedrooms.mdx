## Why Bedroom Air Quality Matters

You spend roughly one-third of your life sleeping, and the air quality in your bedroom directly affects how well you rest. Bedrooms accumulate dust mites in bedding, pet dander on surfaces, and VOCs from furniture and carpeting. Poor air quality during sleep contributes to morning congestion, nighttime coughing, and restless sleep.

The SonoHealth AirPro delivers HEPA 14 filtration at a whisper-quiet 25 dB in Night Mode, continuously cleaning bedroom air without disrupting sleep.

This matters because noise disrupts sleep architecture. Even sounds that do not fully wake you can shift your sleep from deep, restorative stages into lighter phases. The AirPro's Night Mode ensures filtration runs all night without contributing to sleep disruption.

Research consistently shows that air quality affects sleep. Key benefits of filtered bedroom air include:

- **Reduced nighttime congestion** from lower dust mite and pollen exposure
