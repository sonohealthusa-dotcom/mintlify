## Why Indoor Allergens Are a Year-Round Problem

Most people associate allergies with springtime pollen, but indoor allergens circulate 365 days a year. Dust mites thrive in bedding and upholstery. Pet dander accumulates on every surface. Mold spores drift through HVAC systems. Even with regular cleaning, these microscopic particles remain suspended in the air you breathe.

The SonoHealth AirPro addresses this with HEPA 14 filtration that captures 99.9% of airborne particles as small as 0.1 microns, removing allergens that standard air filters miss entirely.

## Common Allergens and How AirPro Captures Them

### Pollen (10-100 microns)

Pollen grains are among the largest airborne allergens, ranging from 10 to 100 microns in diameter. They enter homes through open windows, on clothing, and through HVAC intake vents. The AirPro's HEPA 14 filter captures pollen with near-total efficiency since these particles are well above the filter's 0.1-micron threshold.

### Dust Mites (1-10 microns)

Dust mite allergens are not the mites themselves but their microscopic waste particles, which measure 1 to 10 microns. These particles become airborne when you walk across carpet, sit on upholstered furniture, or make your bed. The AirPro pulls these particles from the air and traps them permanently in the HEPA 14 filter.

### Pet Dander (2.5+ microns)

Pet dander consists of tiny flakes of skin shed by cats, dogs, birds, and other animals with fur or feathers. These particles start at 2.5 microns and are light enough to remain airborne for hours. The AirPro captures pet dander efficiently, making it an effective solution for households with pets and allergy-prone family members.

### Fine Dust Particles (&lt;2.5 microns)

Household dust contains a mixture of skin cells, fabric fibers, insect debris, and soil tracked indoors. The finest dust particles, those under 2.5 microns, are the most dangerous because they penetrate deep into the respiratory system. The AirPro's HEPA 14 filter captures these ultrafine particles that standard HEPA filters cannot.

## 3-Stage Filtration for Complete Allergen Protection

The AirPro uses a layered approach to allergen removal:

1. **Pre-Filter** captures large particles like hair, lint, and visible dust before they reach the primary filter. This extends the life of the HEPA 14 filter and maintains airflow efficiency.

2. **HEPA 14 Filter with Antibacterial Coating** traps 99.9% of particles down to 0.1 microns. The antibacterial coating prevents captured biological allergens from growing or degrading on the filter surface.

3. **Activated Carbon Filter** absorbs volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that trigger allergic reactions and respiratory irritation. Many household products, from cleaning supplies to air fresheners, release VOCs that aggravate allergies.

Beyond particles, many allergy sufferers react to volatile organic compounds released by everyday household products. Cleaning sprays, scented candles, new furniture, fresh paint, and synthetic air fresheners all release VOCs that irritate airways and trigger allergy-like symptoms. The AirPro's activated carbon filter absorbs these chemical compounds, addressing a category of allergens that HEPA filters alone cannot capture.
