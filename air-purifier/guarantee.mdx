## 60-Day Free Trial

Try the AirPro for 60 days. If you are not satisfied for any reason, return it for a **complete refund** -- no questions asked. SonoHealth covers return shipping for defective units.

- Trial period begins on the date of delivery.
- The unit must be returned in its original packaging.

## Standard 2-Year Warranty

Every AirPro includes a **2-year limited warranty** covering:

- Defects in materials and workmanship
- Motor and fan assembly failures
- Electronic control malfunctions
- UV-C lamp defects

The warranty does **not** cover:

- Normal wear on consumable filters (these are replacement items)
- Cosmetic damage from drops or misuse
- Damage caused by use outside the product's specifications
- Units purchased from unauthorized resellers

## Lifetime Warranty (Filter Subscription)

Subscribe to automatic filter replacement delivery and the standard 2-year warranty **upgrades to a lifetime warranty** for the entire duration of your active subscription. You also receive:

- **20% off** every filter order
- **Lifetime filter tracking** -- filters ship automatically before yours expires
- Priority customer support

The lifetime warranty covers the same items as the standard warranty but has no expiration date as long as the subscription remains active.
