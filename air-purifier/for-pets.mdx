## The Air Quality Challenge of Living with Pets

Pet owners face a persistent indoor air quality challenge. Studies show that homes with pets have significantly higher levels of airborne particulate matter than pet-free households. Pet dander, hair, saliva proteins, and odors circulate continuously, and standard cleaning only addresses what settles on surfaces, not what stays suspended in the air.

The SonoHealth AirPro addresses every category of pet-related air pollution through its 3-stage filtration system, capturing particles as small as 0.1 microns with HEPA 14 medical-grade filtration.

## What Pets Put in Your Air

### Pet Dander (2.5+ microns)

Dander is the primary cause of pet-related allergies. These microscopic flakes of skin are shed continuously by cats, dogs, birds, rabbits, and other animals. At 2.5 microns and larger, dander particles are light enough to remain airborne for hours and small enough to penetrate deep into the respiratory system. The AirPro's HEPA 14 filter captures dander with 99.9% efficiency.

### Pet Hair and Fur

Loose pet hair carries dander and saliva proteins that trigger allergic reactions. The AirPro's pre-filter is the first line of defense, catching hair and fur before it reaches the HEPA 14 filter. This protects the primary filter from premature clogging and maintains consistent airflow, which is especially important in heavy-shedding households.

### Pet Odors

The activated carbon filter absorbs pet odors at the molecular level, including urine, litter box, wet dog, and general pet smell. Unlike air fresheners that mask odors with fragrance, the carbon filter removes odor-causing compounds from the air entirely.

### Saliva and Urine Proteins

Cat and dog saliva contains the proteins Fel d 1 and Can f 1 respectively, which are potent allergens. When pets groom themselves, these proteins become airborne on dander particles. The HEPA 14 filter captures these protein-laden particles, and the antibacterial coating prevents them from degrading and releasing allergens on the filter surface.

## 3-Stage Filtration Designed for Pet Homes

Each filtration stage targets a different aspect of pet-related air pollution:

1. **Pre-Filter** catches pet hair, fur, and large dander particles. In homes with heavy shedders like Golden Retrievers, Huskies, or long-haired cats, the pre-filter prevents these large particles from reducing the lifespan of the HEPA 14 filter.

2. **HEPA 14 with Antibacterial Coating** captures fine dander particles at 2.5 microns and smaller, including the ultrafine fragments that cause the most severe allergic reactions. The antibacterial coating prevents captured biological material from degrading on the filter.

3. **Activated Carbon + UV-C** absorbs pet odors and neutralizes bacteria carried on dander and hair. The UV-C function kills airborne pathogens that pets can introduce from outdoor environments.
