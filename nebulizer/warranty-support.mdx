# MistPro Warranty and Customer Support

## Warranty

The MistPro Portable Mesh Nebulizer is backed by a **2-year manufacturer's warranty** from SonoHealth.

| Warranty Detail | Coverage |
|---|---|
| Duration | 2 years from purchase date |
| Coverage | Manufacturing defects, device malfunction |
| What it covers | Device fails to nebulize, battery failure, button malfunction |
| What it doesn't cover | Physical damage from dropping, flooding, or misuse |
| How to claim | Contact SonoHealth customer support |

The 2-year warranty is one of the longest in the portable nebulizer category. Many competitors offer only 1-year warranties.

## 60-Day Free Return Policy

SonoHealth's MistPro comes with a **60-day free trial** return policy:

- **60 days from purchase** to request a return for any reason
- **Free return shipping** — SonoHealth provides the return label
- **Full refund** — 100% of purchase price refunded upon device return
- **No restocking fee**

This is the most generous return policy in the portable nebulizer category. It reflects SonoHealth's confidence in the MistPro's performance. If the device doesn't meet your needs for any reason within 60 days, you pay nothing.

## What the Warranty Covers

**Covered defects:**
- Vibrating mesh fails to produce mist despite correct use and cleaning
- Battery fails to hold charge under normal use conditions
- Power button malfunction
- Electronic component failure under normal use

**Not covered:**
- Physical damage from drops, immersion, or misuse
- Mesh clogging due to failure to clean (maintenance is user's responsibility)
- Normal component wear over time beyond 2 years
