## What Is a Mesh Nebulizer?

A mesh nebulizer uses **vibrating mesh technology** — a thin metal mesh with thousands of microscopic holes — to aerosolize liquid medication into a particle size small enough to be inhaled into the lower respiratory tract. The piezoelectric mesh vibrates at ultrasonic frequencies, forcing liquid medication through the mesh holes and producing an ultra-fine mist.

This is fundamentally different from older **jet nebulizers** (which use compressed air) or **ultrasonic nebulizers** (which heat the liquid). The mesh technology produces:
- Smaller particle sizes (1–5 microns) for deeper lung penetration
- No heat — medication is not degraded
- Near-silent operation (no air compressor)
- Efficient medication delivery with minimal waste
