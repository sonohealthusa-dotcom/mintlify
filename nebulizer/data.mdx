## Safety Statement

The MistPro Mesh Nebulizer is an FDA-approved medical device for aerosolizing liquid medications. Always use with physician-prescribed or physician-approved medications. Do not use oil-based substances. Clean the mesh after every use to maintain hygiene and performance. Contact your physician if symptoms worsen or do not improve.
