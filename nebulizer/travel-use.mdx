## Why MistPro Is the Ideal Travel Nebulizer

Traditional jet nebulizers are impractical for travel — they require a power outlet, include a bulky compressor, and cannot be used on planes. The MistPro is designed from the ground up for portability:

| Feature | Travel Benefit |
|---|---|
| Battery powered | No outlet needed during treatment |
| Pocket-sized | Fits in carry-on bag, purse, or jacket pocket |
| Near-silent | Usable without disturbing other passengers |
| One-button operation | No setup required |
| USB charging | Rechargeable from any standard USB port, power bank, or laptop |

## TSA and Air Travel

### Can I Bring a Nebulizer on a Plane?

Yes. Nebulizers are classified as **medical devices** and are explicitly permitted in carry-on baggage by the TSA. You are not required to count the nebulizer against your carry-on bag limit.

**TSA recommendations for traveling with a nebulizer:**
- Carry the device in your carry-on, not checked baggage (to ensure access and prevent damage)
- Carry physician documentation or prescription for any nebulized medications
- Be prepared to remove the device from your bag for X-ray screening
- Medications may be taken through security in quantities exceeding the normal 3.4oz liquid limit when they are for medical use (declare them separately)

### Using During Flight

The MistPro can be used during a flight. Best practices:
- Use the **mask rather than a mouthpiece** to contain mist within your treatment space
- Perform treatment discreetly — other passengers may be concerned by mist or device sounds if unfamiliar
- Inform a flight attendant before use if you are treating a young child

## Hotel Use

The MistPro's USB charging means you can charge it from any:
- USB wall charger (most hotel rooms)
- USB port on a laptop
- Universal travel adapter + USB charger
- Portable battery/power bank

No voltage converters are required for charging (USB is universally compatible).

## International Travel

The MistPro's USB charging is compatible with international voltage standards (via a standard USB wall adapter). Carry your country-specific adapter for the USB charger if needed.

For international travelers carrying nebulized prescription medications:
- Carry medications in original pharmacy packaging with labels
- Carry a physician's letter stating the medical necessity
- Some countries regulate specific nebulizer medications (check customs regulations for destination)

## Packing the MistPro

**Minimum travel kit:**
- MistPro device
- USB charging cable
- Medication cup and mask (already attached or in the carry case)
- Sufficient saline vials or medication for the trip duration
- Small tube of ultrasound gel (if applicable) — though the MistPro does not require gel

**Store the device:**
- In a clean, sealed bag or small case
- Away from extreme temperatures in checked luggage
- Accessible in carry-on for in-flight use

## For Travelers with Asthma

The MistPro is particularly valuable for asthmatic travelers because:
- Air travel itself can trigger asthma (low cabin humidity, recycled air)
- Hotel environments may have allergen triggers
- Access to local pharmacies for replacement inhalers varies by destination

Having a portable nebulizer with a full supply of albuterol ampules for nebulization provides backup respiratory therapy capability anywhere in the world.
