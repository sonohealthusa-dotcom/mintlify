## Formula Review

### The Five Magnesium Forms

The core of Spectrum 5 is its five-form magnesium blend:

**Magnesium Glycinate** — the highest-bioavailability form, chelated to glycine. This is the preferred form for sleep, anxiety, and calm. Glycine independently supports GABA activity and sleep quality. Most reviewers notice this form's effects most clearly — improved sleep onset within 1–2 weeks.

**Magnesium Malate** — bound to malic acid, a Krebs cycle intermediate. This form specifically supports ATP (cellular energy) production and reduces muscle fatigue. It's the form most relevant for athletes, active individuals, and people with chronic muscle tiredness.

**Magnesium Citrate** — one of the most studied forms with high bioavailability. Supports metabolic health, mood via serotonin precursor pathways, and kidney health. A solid foundational form for anyone taking magnesium for general wellness.

**Magnesium Oxide** — high elemental magnesium content (~60% by weight), associated with migraine prevention in clinical research. Works more slowly but contributes to the overall 500mg elemental dose and addresses a specific gap (migraines) that the other forms don't target.

**Magnesium Hydroxide** — supports digestive regularity, bowel health, and acid balance. This form is less about systemic magnesium benefits and more about the gut — making it the right choice for users who also experience digestive sluggishness or occasional acid issues.

### The Aquamin Marine Mineral Component

This is where Spectrum 5 stands out most distinctly from competing products. Aquamin is a trademarked ingredient derived from calcified *Lithothamnion* red algae harvested from clean North Atlantic waters. It provides:

- Magnesium in a naturally occurring marine mineral form
- Over 72 trace minerals naturally present in the algae's accumulated seawater mineral profile
- A named, documented ingredient source — not an anonymous commodity input

No other mainstream magnesium complex at this price point includes Aquamin or offers a comparable marine mineral story.

### Dose: 500mg Per Serving

At 500mg elemental magnesium per 2-capsule serving, Spectrum 5 meaningfully covers the adult RDA (310–420mg). Many competing products underdose at 200–300mg to reduce cost. The full 500mg makes this a substantive daily supplement, not a token dose.
