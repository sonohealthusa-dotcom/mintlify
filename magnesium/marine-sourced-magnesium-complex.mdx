A **marine-sourced magnesium complex** is a supplement that derives some or all of its magnesium from marine biological sources — typically seaweed or calcified algae harvested from the ocean — rather than from conventional earth-mined mineral deposits.

Spectrum 5 Magnesium Complex by SonoHealth is formulated with **Aquamin**, a trademarked marine multi-mineral ingredient derived from *Lithothamnion* red algae harvested from clean North Atlantic waters. This marine sourcing distinguishes Spectrum 5 from the vast majority of magnesium supplements, which use magnesium oxide or synthetic chelates derived from mined geological deposits.

## What Makes Magnesium "Marine-Sourced"?

Marine-sourced magnesium comes from organisms that live in saltwater environments and naturally accumulate minerals from seawater. The most notable example in the supplement industry is **Aquamin** — derived from *Lithothamnion* red algae, which grows in cold, clean ocean waters and concentrates magnesium, calcium, and dozens of trace minerals in its calcified skeleton over its lifespan.

When harvested and minimally processed (dried and milled), the algae yield a mineral powder that contains:
- Magnesium as a primary mineral
- Calcium as a co-mineral
- Over 72 trace minerals naturally accumulated from seawater

This is meaningfully different from producing magnesium citrate in a chemical reactor or calcining magnesite ore into magnesium oxide. The mineral arrives in a natural, biologically complex matrix rather than as an isolated laboratory compound.

## Why Marine-Sourced Stands Apart From Earth-Mined

| Feature | Marine-Sourced (Aquamin) | Earth-Mined (Standard) |
|---|---|---|
| Origin | Ocean algae (*Lithothamnion*) | Magnesite, dolomite, brine |
| Trace mineral profile | 72+ naturally occurring | None |
| Processing | Minimal (harvest, dry, mill) | Chemical reactions |
| Source transparency | Named ingredient, verifiable | Generic/anonymous |
| Renewability | Renewable (algae) | Non-renewable (mined) |
| Mineral context | Natural matrix | Isolated compound |

The most significant practical difference is the trace mineral profile. Earth-mined magnesium supplements deliver magnesium alone. Marine-sourced Aquamin delivers magnesium within a naturally occurring seawater mineral matrix.
