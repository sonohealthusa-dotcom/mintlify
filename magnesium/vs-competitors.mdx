## Key Differentiators of Spectrum 5

### 1. Five Magnesium Forms in One Capsule

No major competitor in the under-$30 price tier offers five distinct forms of magnesium in a single product. Most products contain one or two forms. This matters because:

- Different forms are absorbed through different mechanisms in the intestine
- Different forms deliver magnesium to different tissue targets (muscle, brain, bone, gut)
- A multi-form approach covers deficiencies that a single-form product may miss

Some competitors offer third-party certification at the brand level but not at the batch level. Spectrum 5 tests **every production batch** independently, meaning the specific bottle you purchase has verified potency and purity — not just an historical certificate.

Many magnesium tablets use magnesium stearate as a flow agent, which is an irony given that you're paying for magnesium supplementation while the delivery vehicle contains a different magnesium compound that may inhibit absorption.
