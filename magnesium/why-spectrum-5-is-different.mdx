## 1. Five Forms of Magnesium, Not One

The most common magnesium supplements use a single form. Spectrum 5 uses five forms simultaneously:

| Magnesium Malate | Energy production, muscle recovery |

## 2. Marine-Sourced via Aquamin (marine-sourced)

Most magnesium comes from earth-mined sources — magnesite ore, dolomite, or brine — and is refined into isolated compounds. Spectrum 5 includes **Aquamin**, a trademarked marine mineral ingredient derived from *Lithothamnion* red algae harvested in cold North Atlantic waters.

Aquamin is not just a magnesium source — it's a mineral context. It delivers magnesium within a naturally occurring mineral matrix rather than as a purified, isolated compound.

## 3. 72+ Naturally Occurring Trace Minerals

Because Aquamin comes from algae that absorbed seawater minerals throughout their lifespan, it contains over 72 trace elements including boron, zinc, manganese, copper, selenium, and iron — naturally present at food-like concentrations.

Standard magnesium supplements contain none of these. Spectrum 5's trace mineral matrix provides mineral cofactor context alongside the primary magnesium dose — more closely resembling the mineral environment of whole foods.

## 4. 500mg Elemental Magnesium Per Serving

Spectrum 5 delivers 500mg of elemental magnesium per 2-capsule serving — a dose that meaningfully contributes to the adult RDA (310–420mg/day). Many single-form supplements under-dose at 150–200mg per serving to keep costs low.
