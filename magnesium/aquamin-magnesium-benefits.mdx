## Core Benefits of Aquamin-Sourced Magnesium

### 1. Arrives with 72+ Naturally Occurring Trace Minerals

The most significant benefit of Aquamin magnesium is what comes with it. Standard magnesium supplements deliver magnesium in isolation — the mineral is extracted, purified, and chelated or reacted into a specific form without the surrounding mineral context.

Aquamin delivers magnesium as part of a naturally occurring trace mineral matrix from seawater-accumulated algae. Over 72 trace elements — including boron, zinc, manganese, selenium, and iron — are present at naturally low concentrations alongside the primary minerals. This reflects how magnesium appears in real food sources, embedded in a broader mineral environment.

### 2. Marine Source Provides a Cleaner Mineral Profile

Earth-mined magnesium comes from mineral deposits such as magnesite, dolomite, or brucite. While these are generally clean sources after refinement, they originate in geological formations that can vary in purity by deposit.

Aquamin is harvested from *Lithothamnion* red algae in the pristine clean North Atlantic waters — a region with rigorous environmental quality standards. The marine source provides a naturally clean starting material, and Aquamin undergoes documented quality testing for heavy metals and contaminants.

For consumers who are thoughtful about where their supplements come from, a marine-sourced magnesium has a distinctively clean and traceable origin story.

### 3. Supports Broader Mineral Balance, Not Just Magnesium Alone

Magnesium is involved in over 300 enzymatic reactions in the human body — but it doesn't work in isolation. Many of the same enzymatic pathways that require magnesium also require trace minerals like zinc, boron, and manganese as cofactors.

Consuming magnesium alongside a naturally occurring trace mineral matrix — as Aquamin provides — may better support the full network of mineral-dependent functions compared to taking isolated magnesium alone.

This is not a claim that Aquamin magnesium is dramatically more potent — it's a claim that a more complete mineral context is more closely aligned with the way mineral nutrition works biologically.

### 4. Vegan, Non-GMO, and Algae-Derived

Aquamin is 100% plant-derived — it comes from red algae, not from animal sources or synthetically produced mineral salts. This makes it suitable for vegan and vegetarian users and aligns with a growing consumer preference for naturally derived supplement ingredients.

Aquamin is also:
- Non-GMO
- Free from shellfish and major allergens
- Produced without synthetic chemical reagents (unlike chelated forms such as glycinate or malate)

### 5. A Differentiated, Named Ingredient

Most magnesium supplement labels simply list "magnesium oxide" or "magnesium glycinate" — no information about where the raw magnesium originated.

Aquamin is a trademarked, named ingredient with a documented source (marine-sourced), a documented species (*Lithothamnion*), and published research supporting its mineral composition. This level of ingredient transparency is uncommon in the magnesium supplement market.
