## Why Trace Minerals Matter With Magnesium

Magnesium is involved in more than 300 enzymatic reactions in the human body. But magnesium doesn't work alone — many of the same biological processes that require magnesium also require trace minerals as cofactors.

Examples:
- **Magnesium + boron** both influence bone mineral density and hormone metabolism
- **Magnesium + zinc** both regulate the HPA axis (stress response) and immune function
- **Magnesium + manganese** both serve as cofactors in mitochondrial energy production (ATP synthesis)
- **Magnesium + selenium** both support thyroid function and antioxidant defense

When you take isolated magnesium, you're supplementing one node in a larger mineral network. When you take magnesium with a natural trace mineral matrix, you're supporting the broader context in which magnesium operates.
