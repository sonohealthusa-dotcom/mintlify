1. Homepage has zero schema — no brand entity

High impact

No Organization or WebSite JSON-LD exists anywhere site-wide. This is what lets ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini recognize "SonoHealth" as an entity and attach your trust signals (Google Top Quality Store 4.7/659, ScamAdviser 100/100, social profiles) via sameAs. Without it, strong product ratings float with no recognized company behind them.

Fix: one snippet in theme.liquid <head>Effort: ~15 minOwner: Claude drafts → you paste

2. Offers omit return policy & shipping

High impact

None of the product offers declare hasMerchantReturnPolicy or shippingDetails — yet you offer free shipping and a 60-day money-back guarantee. These are exactly the attributes agents surface ("free shipping, easy returns") and they also unlock Google's free product listings.

Fix: extend Offer schemaEffort: ~30 minOwner: Claude drafts → you paste

3. llms.txt has a stale price & thin ratings

Medium

llms.txt lists magnesium at $25; the live price is $29. Agents quote llms.txt verbatim, so a wrong price erodes trust. Most catalog entries also omit per-product star ratings — adding "4.82★ / 247 reviews" to each is free social proof in the first file agents read.

Fix: correct price + add ratingsEffort: ~15 minOwner: Claude drafts → you paste in Liquid

Medium
